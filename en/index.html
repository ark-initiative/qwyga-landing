With Qwyga you learn Japanese so that you understand everything. So that you can say what you really mean in one sentence.

You are in control - of your learning and your language

Decide what you learn and how much you learn. You decide what your language course looks like and are therefore fully empowered with your Japanese language skills.

You save your valuable time for the important things

Only learn the things that really interest you. Only go as deep as you really want to.

We've all been there: starting out in a foreign language is hard, and learning it takes time and effort. Japanese, with its writing systems, is certainly no exception. After the initial hurdles, you can finally say your name - but this doesn't create a dialog. You lack the skills to express your thoughts in Japanese - which means you lack the confidence to actually speak Japanese with Japanese people.

In many products for beginners, you are presented with prefabricated sentences or individual words and explanations are reduced to a minimum or do not exist at all. As a result, you don't understand what is actually coming out of your mouth. You have no real control over what you say.
We have therefore developed Qwyga to give you everything you need to learn Japanese seriously and fully.

We can't promise that learning Japanese will be effortless. But we do promise not to waste your time with pointless games.

To get the most out of your learning time, we have developed Qwyga around a few key points:

You have the power!

Tell Qwyga WHAT you want to learn and HOW you want to learn. The app will build you a language course that is as unique as you are.

Everyone learns differently. And everyone has different goals and interests. Qwyga asks you a few questions at the beginning, which you can use to determine your own learning content and the way in which it is taught to you. In concrete terms, this means that you use your precious time for exactly what you want to learn. And you enjoy learning more because you learn what interests you. Gone are the days when you had to adapt to the learning program. When you learn with Qwyga, the app adapts to you.

Incidentally, this even goes so far that you can choose to learn Japanese with or without grammar explanations. Depending on what you choose, your learning experience will be different, but in the end you will learn exactly how you feel comfortable.

Thematic focus

Learn Japanese in a topic-centered way. Whether it's going to a restaurant, studying or Halloween, Qwyga contains topics for many everyday scenarios.

In real life (and you are probably learning for this) you will need Japanese in certain situations: You want to check into a hotel, talk about your hobbies or read a newspaper article about e-mobility in Japan. Different situations require different vocabulary. That's why Qwyga is organized into subject areas that teach you exactly what you need for each situation. And you'll learn how to communicate in Japanese using realistic dialogs.

Three learning levels

Decide for yourself whether you want to start with a broad-based approach to your Japanese or go into more depth on a specific topic, including complex conversations.

Within a subject area, you will find several lessons, each assigned to a learning level (BASIC, INTERMEDIATE, ADVANCED). These are presented to you in a sequence that takes you step by step from simple conversations to the discussion of complex issues. Once you have completed all the BASIC lessons in a topic, you will unlock a new topic. You then have the choice of tackling the new topic at BASIC level or continuing with the old topic at the higher learning levels. Or you can mix and match as you wish. It's your learning, and therefore your choice!

Innovative script introduction

Learn the Japanese script systems by reading and writing from the get-go.

Many Japanese textbooks start with a table of syllabaries, briefly explain the basic principles of how to write with them... and then leave you to learn the script on your own. Qwyga has a learning approach where you discover the script and its principles on your own. One by one, the app reveals the syllabaries to you and lets you read and write words from the very first second. The “learning” happens practically on the side.

And of course Qwyga doesn't just teach you kanji like that. We link the kanji introduction to the vocabulary introduction so that you always have the context in mind for which a kanji is relevant.

Dubbing by native speakers

You hear Japanese as it really sounds.

This allows you to practise listening and repeating right from the start and gain confidence in dealing with the sound of speech. Because with every computer-generated voice, doubts remain as to whether what you hear is really authentic.

So if you're someone who says: "I want to learn Japanese seriously now", then you've found what you're looking for. With Qwyga you learn authentic Japanese. With full control over your learning and your language skills.

Yes, should Qwyga not be to your liking, you can delete your account from the app at any time. Note that you have to cancel your subscriptions manually.

What is your cancellation policy?

  • You can cancel anytime and progress will remain unaffected.
  • If you cancel, you will have access to the contents for the duration of the original subscription.
    Example: You cancel your three month subscription after two months; then you can access the contents for another month.

Can I move my subscription from iOS to Android (or vice versa)?

Unfortunately, this is not possible. Please note the following:

  • You'll continue to be charged on the platform you initially subscribed to. For example, if you signed up while on an iPhone, you'll continue being billed each month, every three months or each year – depending on your subscription – through the App Store.
  • It doesn't matter where you initially started your subscription; your subscriber access stays tied to your user account.

Which payment methods do you accept?

Payment transactions are handled by Google or Apple, depending on the platform you subscribed from.
Therefore, we accept all payment methods specified by Google on Android and all payment methods specified by Apple on iOS.

What if I want to be billed through the platform of my new device?

You'll need to cancel your current subscription on the old platform, wait until it's over, and re-subscribe on the new one.
